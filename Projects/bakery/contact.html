Contact

Please email or call me to setup an appointment for a tasting, or to discuss your custom cake. Feel free to include inspiration photos or ideas in your email, and bring along samples of fabric or other details from your event to your appointment. You can also view the cakes in my showcase to help give you an idea of what we can create together.

For email, please include your full name, telephone number, email address, best time to reach you, your cake occasion, and your celebration date, along with your message.
